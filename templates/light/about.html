What We Do

light is a fun and friendly application that's happy to be here, helping you along on your journey to becoming a smilier, healthier version of you! If you're looking to improve on your wellness with no strings attached, all you need to do is sign-up - it's free!

How We Do It

Whether you prefer to go it alone or collaborate with friends, light has lots of fun features to offer that can help you manage your stress, make the most of your day, set and achieve goals and increase your overall wellbeing through practices of mindfulness and self-reflection.




Plans

Want some help learning how to build great habits and turn them into lifelong ones? light's got you covered - follow one of our custom plans for a step-by-step breakdown of awesome ideas to get you started. And the best part? The plans are tailored to your unique interests, and yours alone. Cool, right?

Challenges

Or if you prefer your fun with fellow lighters, maybe you'd like to check out our challenges instead. Want to see what goals others are setting for themselves? Even better, want to take part and work towards a goal alongside a host of dedicated others to help motivate you on your way? Join one of our challenges, see how other users are progressing through the tasks to meet their goals, or even issue a challenge yourself with tasks created by none other than you! You decide what's important. The only limit is your imagination (and the boundaries of the known scientific world).

Resources

Choose from a fantastic range of available resources -

  • You like to read? Try one of our articles, ranging from the 'how-to's to 'do-not's
  • If you like to consume your content as you go about your day, then maybe a podcast is for you
  • Looking to take your wellness journey even further? Then try one of our linked apps

All of our resources are hand-picked for you. From the start of your journey to the end - we'll be there, helping hands at the ready.

Light is not a clinical or diagnostic tool (no science here), but we still hope that you get something positive out of your experience with us. While it may not be a replacement for mental health treatment, but it can be here whenever you need it. If your mental health - or that of someone you know - is suffering, please reach out for professional support.
